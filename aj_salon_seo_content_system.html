Whatever your style, getting bridal makeup right requires the right products, the right technique, and most importantly — the right team.

Hyderabad weddings blend cultures beautifully. Telugu, Muslim, and Sikh weddings all have their own makeup requirements. At AJ Salon, our artists are trained across all of these styles, so you always get a look that matches your culture, your skin tone, and your outfit.

We only use professional-grade, skin-safe products — including MAC, Kryolan, and Charlotte Tilbury. For Hyderabad's warm and humid climate, we use extra-hold setting sprays and oil-control primers so your makeup stays fresh from the morning ceremony to the evening reception. We always do a skin patch test before your trial.
H2 — How to prepare for your bridal makeup session
Start a skincare routine at least 30 days before your wedding. Drink plenty of water. Get a facial at AJ Salon 10–14 days before the event to improve your skin texture. Avoid waxing or threading within 48 hours of the event. Come for your appointment with a clean, moisturised face and bring any reference photos that inspire your look.
